REGRESSION LU-PROPERTIES

In this project, I utilized Selenium to collect real estate data from Luxembourgian websites. After extensive data cleaning and feature engineering on 1000+ properties, I explored diverse regression techniques. Meticulous hyperparameter tuning helped identify top-performing models. Leveraging advanced ensemble methods such as StackedRegressor and Voting Regressor, I amalgamated model outputs for highly precise housing price predictions. This comprehensive approach ensures robust pricing estimates, optimizing predictive accuracy for real-world applications.

SENTIMENT ANALYSIS LU-NEWS

In this project, I harnessed Selenium for web scraping, gathering 500+ recent news articles from Luxembourg. This dataset, including titles and descriptions, was pivotal for evaluating various sentiment analysis methods, from conventional machine learning to deep learning models. Similar to the regression task, I conducted sentiment analysis on both title and description sections independently, measuring sentiment intensity. Then, employing a comprehensive approach, I aggregated these scores to provide a nuanced portrayal of the news content.

RECOMMENDATION LU-ITINERARY

In this project, I meticulously gathered data on Luxembourg's top museums, galleries, attractions, landmarks, and parks, including Google ratings and brief descriptions. Utilizing this dataset, I devised an itinerary generator employing a sophisticated algorithm to match user preferences with destination attributes. Tailoring the itinerary to the user's travel duration, I optimized for diversity and coherence, considering practical constraints. The itinerary also incorporates breaks for meals and leisurely dinners, ensuring a balanced travel experience.

CLASSIFICATION LU-FOOD

In this project, I curated a dataset of the top 7 Luxembourgish dishes, facing challenges due to limited imagery. Using this dataset, I developed a sophisticated food recognition system with the VGGNet Model. Additionally, I designed a complex Mixed model to extract key features for accurate dish identification. As an innovative addition, the system provides users with dish recognition, concise descriptions, and recipes, aiming to enhance awareness and appreciation of Luxembourgish cuisine and inspire further exploration in related domains.
